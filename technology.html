How the engine works

Client SDKs → AI detection → Fingerprint & on-chain proof → Verify anywhere.

1) Upload

User/app submits media (or hash) for checking.

2) AI Analysis

Detector ensemble inspects artifacts, compression residuals, and edits.

3) Fingerprint & Proof

Cryptographic fingerprint + model version anchored on-chain.

4) Verify Anywhere

Anyone can re-check media against the on-chain record.

Privacy-first: only fingerprints and minimal metadata are stored—never raw media.
